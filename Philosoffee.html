Philosophy!

PHILOSOFFEE began when a boy from the coast of Kerala fell in love with a girl from the mountains of Coorg.

The rest is told through our coffee.

We brew exclusively with 100% Arabica from select shade-grown estates in Coorg, cultivated above 3,000 feet and medium roasted to bring out balanced acidity, rich aroma, and natural sweetness.

Every cup is crafted with care—whether brewed by hand or pulled as espresso—following international standards to let the coffee speak for itself.

The Arabica

We've brewed exclusively with 100% Arabica since the day we opened in 2021.

Why?

Because we found it to be the coffee best suited for everyday drinking. We believed our community deserved it. We loved it ourselves, so it was the only coffee we wanted to serve.

Every cup at PHILOSOFFEE is brewed using single-origin, shade-grown, naturally processed, medium-roasted Arabica—nothing else.

A coffee made to be enjoyed, not just occasionally, but every day.

Business

We also offer coffee consulting for those who dream of starting their own café or building a coffee brand.

From sourcing quality coffee beans and designing your bar to developing recipes, brewing standards, staff training, and shaping your café concept, we guide you through every stage of the journey.

Having built and operated profitable small specialty coffee cafés ourselves, we understand what it takes to make coffee work as both a craft and a business. Our focus is on helping you create efficient, profitable specialty cafés that serve exceptional coffee without unnecessary complexity.
