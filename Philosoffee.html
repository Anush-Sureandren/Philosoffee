For the Love of Coffee

At Philosoffee, we don't just brew coffee: we honour the journey from the soil to the soul. Our beans are born in the high-altitude mists of Coorg, grown at 3,000 ft where the elevation shapes a denser, more complex flavour profile.

Dedicated to the craft of the bean, we select only 100% Arabica, using a natural process to preserve the inherent sweetness and fruit-forward character of the cherry. Each harvest is small-batch roasted with precision, ensuring that the single-origin story of the Western Ghats is told in every pour. Whether you're seeking a moment of stillness or the perfect rhythm for your day, it all starts here - with a cup built on passion and precision.

Locate us : Thrissur | Bengaluru

Founder of Speciality Coffee &
Open Club Coffee Culture in Irinjalakuda

Philosoffee in Irinjalakuda is the town's pioneer of specialty coffee, introducing a new standard of brewing to the local community. Designed as an open club with a sophisticated bistro setup, our space is a dedicated sanctuary for the craft of the bean.

Our coffee-centric menu is built on a foundation of 100% Arabica beans, sourced from the high-altitude estates of Coorg. We celebrate the versatility of coffee through a range of brewing methods, from the ritual of Pour Over and Aeropress to the patience of our signature Cold Brew and precision-pulled Espresso-based classics.

Steeped in an atmosphere of rock music and a curated library, Philosoffee offers an ambient escape where culture and caffeine collide. Whether you're here to discover a new roast or lose yourself in a book, we invite you to join the revolution.

An original Counter Cafe in Bengaluru

In the heart of the city, Philosoffee Bengaluru reimagines the coffee experience through our original counter café concept. Moving away from the rushed 'grab-and-go' culture, we've designed a compact, intimate space that invites you to slow down.

This is a small café concept where the barrier between guest and craft is removed. With a layout centered around an order counter and cozy seating, it's a space built for connection - making it easy to communicate with your barista about the nuances of our 100% Arabica beans and the story behind each brew.

There is no rush here. We invite you to sit, sip, and enjoy a time of stillness. Whether you are exploring our manual brews or a classic espresso, every cup is an invitation to pause and appreciate the art of specialty coffee.

Coffee Consulting

Beyond our cafes, we offer comprehensive coffee consulting services for those who dream of starting their own cafe, building a unique coffee brand, or opening a Philosoffee branch. From selecting high-quality beans to establishing rigorous brewing standards and shaping your overall cafe concept, we provide guidance every step of the way.

Our team is led by Manu Mohan, the founder of Philosoffee and a professional barista with deep expertise in brewing and bar management. With a strong focus on quality and consistency, we help you build a coffee space rooted in specialty standards.

At the heart of everything we do is our motto, "Way More Coffee". We believe in sharing the true taste of unadulterated, single-origin 100% Arabica - simple, honest, and crafted with care.
